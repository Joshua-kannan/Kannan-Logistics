Services

Services

Discover the range of services provided by Kannan Logistics

Sea Freight

We offer ocean freight services via FCL & LCL groupage to customers all around the world. Our strong relationships with different global shipping lines allow us to give you low-cost solutions without compromising quality.

Air Freight

We have offices strategic partners globally, which enables us to frequently move consoles from Italy, Germany, the USA, China, and Hong Kong into India and the Middle East.

Land Freight

We offer comprehensive land freight options to meet your needs - from rail & road Freight Transporting (FTL) / Less Than Truck Load (LTl) shipping to experienced truckload deliveries.

Project Cargo Logistics

We have in house chartering desks and are the commercial booking agents for fixing all kinds of Break Bulk and Project Cargoes for the group-owned and chartered vessel options, operating Break Bulk sailings in Inter Gulf and Indian Subcontinent regions.

Customs Clearance

We provide a comprehensive customs clearance service for all your import and export requirements. Get assistance with customs clearance and documentation for all your import & export requirements.

Supply Chain Management

We provide comprehensive supply chain management services for businesses looking to optimize their supply chains and reduce their overall costs.

Multimodal Transportation

Our global network combined with our customized solutions enables us to deliver your consignment in a timely and cost-effective manner through multiple modes of transport such as ocean, air and land.

Third-Party Logistics

We have ventured into warehousing & distribution business (3PL) to provide cost-effective, efficient and innovative warehouse solutions. We offer end to end solutions for different industries which encompasses supply chain design, warehousing design, business process, resource optimization, value added distribution services along with optimization to our customers.

Warehousing and Distribution

Our warehousing units are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients. With more than 200,000 square meters of warehousing space located at strategic locations around the globe, we are well equipped to handle the storage and distribution needs of businesses of all sizes
